Building a Python Community Where Everyone Belongs

The Python Diversity & Inclusion Workgroup aims to ensure the Python community reflects the diversity of the world around us, and that all members feel welcome to participate.

Get Involved

What We Believe

Our existence is to move from aspiration to action.

Python is for everyone.

We emphasize including groups that have been historically marginalized or excluded from technology and open source communities.

Diversity

Our community is enriched by the variety of backgrounds, identities, and experiences that participants bring to the table.

Inclusion

It is not enough to just invite people to the table; we must ensure they have the support and resources to thrive once they are here.

Core Values

Our Values Guide Everything We Do

Inclusion

Actively creating space for marginalized groups.

Integrity

Honesty about our own shortcomings and improvements.

Equity

Recognizing that equal treatment is not always fair; providing extra support where needed.

Empathy

Listening first to understand different lived experiences.

Respect

Treating all community members with equal dignity.

Belonging

Not just inviting people in, but making them want to stay.
