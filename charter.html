Transparency matters. Here's how the D&I Workgroup operates.

Our Charter

The Diversity and Inclusion Workgroup was established by PSF Board resolution in November 2020.

Our Purpose: To further the PSF's mission to "support and facilitate the growth of a diverse and international community of Python programmers" and to provide guidance to the PSF Board of Directors in line with this mandate.

We are empowered to:

  • Make recommendations to the PSF Board on D&I matters
  • Conduct research and gather community feedback
  • Develop resources and best practices
  • Represent the workgroup in PSF and community spaces
