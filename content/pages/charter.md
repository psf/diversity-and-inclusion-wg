    <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-gray-900 mb-4">Our Charter</h2>
    <p class="text-gray-600 leading-relaxed mb-4">The Diversity and Inclusion Workgroup was established by PSF Board resolution in November 2020.</p>
    <p class="text-gray-600 leading-relaxed mb-6"><strong>Our Purpose:</strong> To further the PSF's mission to "support and facilitate the growth of a diverse and international community of Python programmers" and to provide guidance to the PSF Board of Directors in line with this mandate.</p>
    <p class="text-gray-600 leading-relaxed mb-4">We are empowered to:</p>
    <ul class="space-y-3 mb-8">
        <li class="flex items-start gap-3 text-gray-600">
            <i class="fa-solid fa-check text-pyblue mt-1 text-sm" aria-hidden="true"></i>
            <span>Make recommendations to the PSF Board on D&amp;I matters</span>
        </li>
        <li class="flex items-start gap-3 text-gray-600">
            <i class="fa-solid fa-check text-pyblue mt-1 text-sm" aria-hidden="true"></i>
            <span>Conduct research and gather community feedback</span>
        </li>
        <li class="flex items-start gap-3 text-gray-600">
            <i class="fa-solid fa-check text-pyblue mt-1 text-sm" aria-hidden="true"></i>
            <span>Develop resources and best practices</span>
        </li>
        <li class="flex items-start gap-3 text-gray-600">
            <i class="fa-solid fa-check text-pyblue mt-1 text-sm" aria-hidden="true"></i>
            <span>Represent the workgroup in PSF and community spaces</span>
        </li>
    </ul>
