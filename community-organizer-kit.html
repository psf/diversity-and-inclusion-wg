The Community Organizer Kit

Practical tools and resources to help you build inclusive Python communities—no matter where you are or how much experience you have.

Why This Exists

Starting a Python community is exciting. Making it inclusive? That's harder.

Organizers often want to do better but don't know where to start. They're figuring things out from scratch, making mistakes others have already learned from, and burning out in the process.

The Community Organizer Kit exists to change that.
